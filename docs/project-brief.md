# Project Brief

## Goal

Build a unified digital platform to manage and sell hot air balloon trips in Luxor for roughly 32 balloon companies through one modern booking system.

## Required business capabilities

- Multi-company operations in one system with isolated dashboards
- B2C and B2B pricing with future contract-based pricing logic
- Reservation creation, payment tracking, voucher issuance, and ticket delivery
- Central admin dashboard plus operator, agency, and support dashboards
- Payment gateway integration
- Arabic and English support from the first release
- Future API readiness for mobile apps and third-party partners

## Target MVP

- Operator onboarding and company-level permissions
- Trip inventory and seat management
- Reservation workflow with passenger details
- Voucher and booking reference generation
- Payment status handling and finance exports
- Reports for daily operations and sales summaries
- Foundational connectors for later integration with travel systems
